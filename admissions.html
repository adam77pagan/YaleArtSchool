Admissions

Yale School of Art is an emerging research university and one of the fastest-growing universities in Connecticut. Our top-tier university currently offers over 100 graduate programs across our college.

Excellence, innovation, and diversity are abundant at our university. A prime example of this is detailed in our current ranking as one of the best young universities in the nation by Times Higher Education. Yale School of Art has also been recognized as a Global Leader and highly ranked as the best University for Veterans by Military Times and “Best of the Best” U.S. universities for Hispanics, African-Americans and Females by the Hispanic Network Magazine.
